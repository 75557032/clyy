迁移驱动：
1.新内核去掉了__devexit，__devinitdata，__devinit，__devexit_p四个宏定义，如果有的话，自己加上即可
#define __devexit
#define __devinitdata
#define __devinit
#define __devexit_p
2.新内核的驱动结构发生变化，以前为：
static struct file_operations adc_fops = {
	.owner	=	THIS_MODULE,
	.open	=	adc_open,
	.write	=	adc_write,
	.read	=	adc_read,
	.release=	adc_release,
	.ioctl	=	adc_ioctl,
};
